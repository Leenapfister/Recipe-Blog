Twisted Fruit Greek Salad

Ingredients

1/4 extra virgin olive oil 1/3 cup strawberry balsamic vinegar 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon pepper 1 large cucumber 1/2 cup kalamata olives 4 large sliced tomatoes 1 large sliced onion 4 cups romaine lettuce 4 ounces diced or crumbled feta cheese 1 zested lemon 4 cups sliced strawberries 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Recipe

1. Mix all wet ingredients together to make the dressing. 2. Chop dry ingredients. Toss into a bowl and mix well. 3. Add dressing to mixed dry ingredients.
