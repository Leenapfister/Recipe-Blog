Veggie Stir Fry

Ingredients

1 tablespoon oil
1 medium onion, sliced thin
1 cup diagonally sliced carrots
2 cups broccoli florets
2 cups sugar snap peas
1 large red bell pepper, cut into strips
1 tablespoon reduced sodium soy sauce
garlic powder
ground ginger
toasted sesame seeds

Recipe (requires stovetop)

1. Heat the oil in a wok or large deep skillet on medium-high heat.

2. Add the onion and carrots; stir fry for 2 minutes.

3. Add the remaining stir fry vegetables; stir fry them for 5 to 7 minutes or until vegetables are tender-crisp.

4. Add soy sauce, garlic powder and ginger; stir fry until well blended.
5. Sprinkle in sesame seeds. Serve over cooked rice, if desired.
