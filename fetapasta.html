Feta Pasta

Ingredients

Pasta shape of choice (short, tubular is recommended) 8 oz block of feta cheese 10 oz rigatoni sauce 1 clove of garlic 2 pints cherry tomatoes 1/2 cup exra virgin olive oil 1/4 cup sliced basil leaves Pinch of salt, black pepper, and/or crushed red pepper flakes

Recipe (requires oven and stovetop)

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. 2. In a medium bowl, toss in cherry tomates, olive oil, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and a several grinds of black pepper. Mix until combined. 3. Transfer items in medium bowl to a large baking dish. Place block of feta in the center and season with pepper. 4. Place baking dish into oven to cook for 30 minutes. Check that the tomatoes have burst and the feta has softened. Then, bake for 10-15 more minutes. 5. Boil salted water on high heat. Add pasta and cook until al dente. Save 1/2 cup cooking water and drain the rest. 6. When finished cooking, remove baking dish from oven. Stir in garlic and red pepper flakes (optional). Smash tomatoes and feta until they become a smooth, creamy sauce. 7. Add pasta to baking dish and stir. If too thick, add cooking water to dilute. Add basil intermittently and add a pinch of salt. Serve with remaining basil and seasoning of choice.
