Old Fashioned Egg Salad Sandwich



Ingredients

4 pre-hardboiled eggs
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/8 cup chopped green onion
1/2 teaspoon yellow mustard
1/8 teaspoon paprika
Pinch of salt and pepper


Recipe

1. Chop pre-hardboiled eggs into small pieces.

2. Toss chopped eggs into a bowl. Stir in mayonnaise, green onion, and
yellow mustard.

3. Season bowl with paprika, salt, pepper, and other seasonings of your choice.
Stir.

4. Spread eggsalad onto preferred bread and serve two full sandwiches.

Optional: May add lettuce for crunch.
